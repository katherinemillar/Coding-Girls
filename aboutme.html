About Coco

Hey! My name is Chloe. I decided to teach myself how to digitally draw during the dreaded year 2020. It was a tough year but there were also some highlights and this small business was one of mine! I started selling custom illustration prints for commission after getting requests. I am in the fourth year of my Product Design & Innovation course. I use Adobe Draw for my prints and create prints mostly of people but also some animals or special things. Check out my instagram @cocoillustrations_ where it all began!
